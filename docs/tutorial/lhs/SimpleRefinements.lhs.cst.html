Refinements are logical formulas

If

  • refinement of T1 implies refinement of T2

  • p1 implies p2

Then

  • T1 is a subtype of T2

  • {v:t | p1} <: {v:t | p2}

Refinements are logical formulas

For example, since

  • v = 0 implies v >= 0

Therefore

  • {v:Int | v = 0} <: {v:Int | v >= 0}
